## Listing Description

> Tucked quietly away from the bustle of downtown, 24 Treford Place is a rare west-end offering - a landmark urban villa in the heart of Trinity Bellwoods and Little Italy. Offered as a luxury short-term furnished lease, this detached residence pairs an authentic industrial studio with a serene, California-inspired home, creating a one-of-a-kind live/work retreat.
>
> The industrial volume is dramatic and light-filled, with soaring ceilings, polished concrete floors, and oversized windows framing the garden beyond. A generous open-concept layout allows for effortless living and entertaining - a chef's kitchen overlooking a dining area for twelve, and a living space anchored by a wood-burning fireplace. Above, a lofted office offers an inspiring place to work or create.
>
> Beyond - either connected seamlessly through the studio or accessed independently from the garden - the home unfolds as a calm, light-washed sanctuary. Wall-to-wall windows open to a beautifully enclosed garden complete with mature landscaping and a wood-burning sauna. Inside, multiple living areas and the thoughtful separation of space provide flexibility for family life or hosting guests.
>
> Private two-car parking with EV charger included. Zoned HVAC and radiant in-floor heating ensure year-round comfort. Steps to College Street, Ossington, Kensington Market, and Trinity Bellwoods Park, yet remarkably peaceful and private, this is a singular opportunity to experience refined west-end living in a truly distinctive and refined architectural setting.
