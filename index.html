The Space

Where Industrial Spirit
Meets California Calm

A rare west-end offering—a landmark urban villa that pairs an authentic industrial studio with a serene, California-inspired home. Soaring ceilings, polished concrete floors, and wall-to-wall windows create spaces that breathe with light and possibility.

The Studio

Dramatic and light-filled, the industrial volume features soaring ceilings, polished concrete floors, and oversized windows framing the garden beyond. A wood-burning fireplace anchors the living space, while a lofted office offers an inspiring place to work or create.

The Kitchen

A chef's dream overlooking a generous dining area for twelve. Open-concept layout allows for effortless living and entertaining, where the boundary between cooking and gathering dissolves into one continuous experience.

The Sanctuary

Beyond the studio—connected seamlessly or accessed independently from the garden—the home unfolds as a calm, light-washed retreat. Multiple living areas and thoughtful separation provide flexibility for family life or hosting guests.

The Garden

A Private Oasis
in the Heart of the City

Wall-to-wall windows open to a beautifully enclosed garden complete with mature landscaping. Here, indoor and outdoor living merge seamlessly— a California-inspired retreat tucked quietly away from the bustle of downtown.

Wood-Burning Sauna

Nestled within the mature garden, a traditional wood-burning sauna offers a moment of ritual and restoration. Step from steam to greenery—a wellness experience that honors both body and landscape.
